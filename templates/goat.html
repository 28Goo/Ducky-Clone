The Goat Edition

The Ducky Year of the Goat edition mechanical keyboard builds on the minimalist, yet modern design with the thinnest bezel ever produced by Ducky.

The YOTG includes all the insane backlighting effects from Ducky - modes such as breathing, colour cycling, wave, snake marquee, raindrop, reactive, aurora and ripple.

The Best Made in Germany Cherry MX RGB Switches

Ducky uses only genuine Cherry MX switches, and its life cycle for over 50 million times.

Standard cross-shaped, allow users to replace keycaps easilty.

Two-stage Feet

Ducky Year of The Goat Edition with new designed that provided two-stage feet and it is different from the traditional monolithic feet.

Users are able to change the angle for the keyboard with the choice of three (foot folded, small foot and big feet)

Unique Serial Number

Only 2015 pieces in existence, thus extremely rare. Each limited keyboard’s inscription plate is number registered to ensure each keyboard’s uniqueness and adding more collecting value.
