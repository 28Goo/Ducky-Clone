The Monkey Edition

The Ducky Year of the Monkey is a limited edition RGB mechanical keyboard that features a two colour anodised finish with a high quality aluminium top case, side LED lighting, Ducky Colour Palette, a built-in flexible mouse bungee, USB N-key rollover, a 1000Hz report rate and USB repeat acceleration.

Built with German Cherry MX switches, the YOTM features RGB lighting that's brighter than ever before thanks to the 3528 SMD RGB LEDs, along with all the insane backlighting effects from Ducky including rain drop mode, 100% full backlight, wave and colour cycling. Limited to 2017 pieces worldwide.
